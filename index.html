Solid‑State Sintering Grain‑Growth Simulation

This spin‑off project (in collaboration with the Chair and Institute for Materials Applications in Mechanical Engineering, RWTH Aachen) recreates the two‑dimensional multiphase‑field model proposed by Wang et al. (Acta Materialia 53 (2005) 2759‑2770). It captures solid‑state sintering and grain coarsening through:

  • The Cahn–Hilliard equation for the evolution of the density field c.
  • The Allen–Cahn equation for the evolution of grain‑orientation order parameters ηi.
  • Rigid‑body translation and rotation of each grain by integrating the net force and torque generated at interfaces.

I implemented the governing equations in MATLAB, ran a test‑case simulation with four initial grains, and recorded the results to visualise densification and pore shrinkage.
