Project Overview

This page summarizes a small research project with IWM (Chair and Institute for Materials Applications in Mechanical Engineering) at RWTH Aachen University that recreates the two‑dimensional multiphase‑field model proposed by Wang et al. (Acta Materialia 53 (2005) 2759‑2770). The model captures solid‑state sintering and grain coarsening using:

  • The Cahn–Hilliard equation for the evolution of the density field c.
  • The Allen–Cahn equation for the evolution of grain‑orientation order parameters ηi.
  • Rigid‑body translation and rotation of each grain by integrating the net force and torque that arise from inter‑grain interactions.

I translated the original variational equations into MATLAB, ran the test case simulation with four initial grains, and recorded the results to visualise densification and pore shrinkage.
